# Slide 1 — Motivation

## Motivation

Modern SOC environments face major challenges:

- massive alert volumes
- limited explainability
- delayed incident response
- fragmented security visibility
- lack of realtime AI assistance

Traditional systems often:

- generate false positives
- lack interpretability
- provide poor analyst guidance
- miss realtime threat evolution

---

# Slide 2 — Research Objective

## Research Objective

Develop an AI-driven SOC platform capable of:

- realtime anomaly monitoring
- explainable threat detection
- SOC analyst assistance
- MITRE ATT&CK mapping
- realtime cyber intelligence visualization
